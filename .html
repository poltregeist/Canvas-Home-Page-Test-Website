Explore the Future of Work

 

Track 1

Career Readiness in the Curriculum 

This track guides educators on integrating career preparedness into academic programs. It covers designing and assessing curricula with career readiness tools, interdisciplinary application, and collaboration with career centers. Practical sessions, hands-on workshops, and a panel discussion with employers and alumni provide actionable strategies for enhancing student success.

 

View Track Sessions

 

Track 2

Career Readiness through Campus Student Employment 

This track teaches incorporating NACE Career Readiness Competencies into student employment, covering skills like communication, teamwork, and digital literacy. Participants learn practical strategies for competency development, focusing on initiatives like Iowa GROW® and guided reflection. The approach enhances student employment as a high-impact activity and equips managers with tools for creating a learning-centric work environment.
